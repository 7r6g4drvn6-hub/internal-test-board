Update rule

Twice-Weekly Update Guidance

  • Update versions: HCP3, HCP5, flash timing, and target version plan.
  • Update resources: VIN, bench owner, booking status, and available window.
  • Update status: available, pending booking, occupied, pending version, or missing information.

Before booking

Before Booking Checklist

  • Confirm the test purpose, expected time window, required model, and environment.
  • Make sure the bench or vehicle has no booking conflict, the owner is known, and logs can be exported.
  • Notify the maintainer by email whenever the version or bench status changes.
